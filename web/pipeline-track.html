Track identity

How it works: after something is detected, the tracker gives it an ID and matches its next position to the nearest old position.

Child version: if two toy cars are moving, we put sticker #1 on one and sticker #2 on the other, then keep the stickers with the same cars.

The webcam demo asks for camera permission and works on HTTPS or localhost. It tracks the centre of changed pixels, not a full object detector.
