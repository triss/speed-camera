Browser-first place observation

A difference engine for a place

Point a recycled phone at one fixed view and lookout logs what comes, goes, and changes there over time. Camera processing and observation storage stay on the device; sharing is explicit and uses findings or exported observation files, not footage by default.

Uses

Vehicle speedSpeed findings from calibrated ground-plane measurements. Counts & footfallCrossing totals, direction split, and flow rate. Dwell & occupancyVisits, dwell time, and simple occupancy findings. Wildlife loggingAppearances, rough size classes, and time-of-day patterns. Environmental changeSlow scene changes against a reference view.
