How this works

Motion log, not a cloud camera. lookout compares small frames on this device, watches for sustained motion in the whole view, and stores timestamped observations. If still capture is enabled, it also saves one JPEG when motion triggers an event.

Use it for a doorway, shed, yard, hallway, gate, or shared room where you want a local record of when something moved. The app does not identify people, faces, or vehicles. It records motion events and optional stills for later review.

  1. Set the phone somewhere stable with a clear view of the area. A shelf, tripod, window ledge, or fixed mount works best.
  2. Press Start camera. The page asks for fullscreen, starts the camera, and begins watching immediately.
  3. Do not move, rotate, or bump the phone once it is watching. If the view changes, pause and restart the camera so the detector settles again.
  4. Use Stills to review captured images. Use Share data only when you deliberately want to export observations or stills.

Fix common problems in Settings. If shadows, rain, leaves, or screen reflections trigger too many events, raise Sensitivity threshold, raise Minimum size, or raise Minimum motion duration. If repeated movement creates too many images, raise Event cooldown. If motion is missed, lower Sensitivity threshold or Minimum motion duration. If the phone is slow, lower Target FPS or Resolution.

Stills may contain identifying imagery. They stay in browser storage on this device and are only included when you explicitly share stills or a bundle.
